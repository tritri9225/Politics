    All About Absentee Ballots

    So what are they?

    An Absentee Ballot is paper slip [most currently an online form] that you can fill out checkin whom you'd like to vote for and you cast the vote by mailing it to your county's election office. This maybe a less inconvenient way to voter instead of going to the polls. You can click the button below to apply for an absentee ballot online. Happy Voting!
