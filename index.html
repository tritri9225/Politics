Why Should You Vote?

You should vote because who becomes elected as president effects your daily life. If you do not vote, you give up your voice. Every voice is important, and your vote counts. Voting is an oppurtunity for change. If you aren't happy with how the US government is being run, you have the ability to help change that by voting!
